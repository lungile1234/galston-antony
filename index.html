About Galston

Builder. Systems Thinker. Future Leader of Johannesburg.

Not a Politician. A Builder.

I am a creative entrepreneur and systems builder with a background in business, infrastructure, media, and property development. As the host of G2G Podcast and mayoral candidate for Johannesburg 2026, I approach government like a business: with clear budgets, KPIs, timelines, and a relentless focus on delivery.

My journey has taught me that complex urban challenges require holistic, sustainable solutions. Johannesburg's problems are interconnectedcrime affects investment, which affects employment, which affects housing. We need systems thinking, not political theater.
